    description: >
      Quilt Shelter is a blanket- or cape-like wearable that explores
      the relationship between sensory perception, memory, and sound.

      The project combines different tactile surfaces that users can touch
      both from the outside and the inside of the garment. These interactions
      trigger contrasting sonic experiences, ranging from annoying high-pitch
      sounds to soft, healing tones.

      The aim is to connect physical sensation with emotional memory,
      allowing users to associate present interactions with past auditory
      experiences.
