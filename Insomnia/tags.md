    description: >
      The concept originated firstly from the material we had at hand,
      and secondly from our prior personal experiences. It is inspired
      by issues of anxiety and depression that we both have experienced.

      Scanning the samples recorded in preparation for the workshop,
      we discovered that we had both captured white noise, rain, and fire
      sounds from our home environments. These are sounds we consider
      soothing and helpful in dealing with an overactive mind that
      prevents rest.

      We decided to upcycle the dress that Martina had brought, both
      because it provided enough material for creating a pillowcase
      and because its pattern visually suited the intended outcome.

      The choice of embroidery for the pressure-sensitive patches was
      inspired by an interactive e-textile example presented during the
      “getting to know the materials” session. It was also influenced by
      Martina’s familiarity with the embroidery technique.
